How It Works

Three steps. That's it.

Whether you're posting a job or looking for work, Chores gets you from zero to done — fast, secure, and simple.

01

Post or Browse

Posters create a listing with a description, pay, date, and address in under two minutes. Workers browse nearby jobs on a live map and apply to what fits their schedule.

02

Match & Hire

Posters review applicants, read profiles and ratings, then hire. Payment is held securely in escrow — the address is only revealed to the hired worker.

03

Complete & Get Paid

Once both parties confirm completion, funds release automatically. Workers receive payment within 24 hours, every single time — no chasing, no delays.

The Story

Built from a
door-to-door hustle.

At 14, I wanted to build a gaming computer. My parents had a simple answer: if you want it, go earn it yourself. So I went door to door through my neighborhood, knocking on strangers' doors and asking if there was anything I could do to make a few dollars.

Six months later, I had enough to build it. But something stuck with me. Going door to door worked — but it was inefficient, awkward, and honestly, I was doing work for neighbors who felt sorry for me rather than for people who genuinely needed help.

I knew there had to be a better way. A way to connect people who truly needed tasks done with people who genuinely wanted to work — with real pay, real accountability, and none of the awkwardness. That's what Chores is.

"There had to be a better way to earn money while helping people who actually needed it."
