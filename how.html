FAQ

Common questions.

Is Chores free to join?
Yes — completely free to create an account and start using the app. Chores charges an 8% platform fee on completed jobs, deducted from the worker's payout. There are no subscription fees or hidden charges.
How does escrow work?
When a poster hires a worker, the payment is captured by Stripe and held in escrow — it's not released to the worker yet. Once both parties confirm the job is complete, the funds are automatically released to the worker within 24 hours. If the worker doesn't show, the poster receives a full refund.
Who can work on Chores?
Workers must be at least 16 years old with a valid government-issued ID or driver's license. Workers under 18 need parental consent. To receive payouts, workers connect a bank account through Stripe — a quick, secure process.
Is my address shared publicly?
No. When you post a job, workers only see an approximate pin on the map — about half a mile from your actual location. Your full address is only revealed to the specific worker you hire, after payment is secured in escrow.
What if something goes wrong?
Either party can open a dispute through the app. Our team reviews every dispute fairly and confidentially within 24 hours. Escrow funds are held until the dispute is resolved, so neither party is left without recourse.
How do I get paid as a worker?
Connect your bank account through Stripe Connect in the app — it takes about 5 minutes. Once both parties confirm job completion, your earnings are automatically deposited within 24 hours. You can set your payout schedule to daily, weekly, or monthly.
What areas does Chores cover?
Chores is available in all states. The app is hyperlocal — jobs are matched by zip code and distance, so you only see and post jobs that are actually near you.
